Our Story

brewed with passion, rooted in manila.

founded in 2026, kapetan started with a simple vision: to elevate the everyday filipino coffee experience. we source premium beans directly from local and international growers, ensuring every cup pays homage to the hard work of traditional farmers.

whether you are looking for a morning jolt, a quiet space to study for your projects, or a cozy corner to connect with friends, we craft space and flavor uniquely for you.

what keeps us brewing

we hold our standards high so your coffee mugs can stay full of genuine flavor.

quality first

from meticulous bean selection to exact espresso extraction parameters, we never compromise on the final cup.

community center

we aim to be more than a shop. KapeTan is a neighborhood anchor where community conversations flow freely.

sustainably minded

by partnering directly with local growers, we keep our supply lines transparent and support ethical farming.
